Welcome to Pretty Picky

My adventures living with the world's pickiest wife.

My wife is severly introverted, her sentences short; her words few. Luckily for me, when she does speak she chooses to be either insightful or brutally honest. She is also a very picky eater who subsists a diet largely consisting of chicken. Needless to say she's a cheap date; she doesn't like steak and she won't touch seafood (She'll never order the "Surf and Turf"). The purpose of this blog is to entertain readers with either her brutal reviews or my missory.

Dewey's Pizza

If it isn't Dewey's Don't Bother

My wife loves Dewey's... so much so that when either of us is feeling pizza I know what to expect. Don't get me wrong, this isn't a complaint, Dewey's is very good. The problem is that our closest Dewey's is 26 minutes away, on a good day with no traffic. This is much too far to ever bring a pizza home too. According to my wife the pizza will always be cold and it won't be nearly as good heated up again in the oven.

Official Review:

Favorite pizza. Has a great crust.

Five Guys

The Best Burger I've Had So Far

Although my wife doesn't like beef, she likes ground beef. Yes, this system is complicated, there's a reason I need a website to track it. Ground beef good, all other beef bad. She has ordered a cheeseburger from just about anywhere they sell them. It's like that episode of How I Met Your Mother where Marshall is searching for the "Best Burger in New York" except, for my wife, it's her entire life.

Official Review:

Five Guys has the best burger. Hi-Pointe Drive-In has a good burger too and I like their fries better than Five Guys

Dairy Queen

The Queen of Dairy

To be given the crown for "Best Ice Cream" from someone with a sweet tooth like my wife is quite the honor. However, one key point is that much of what's considered "Ice Cream" is not ice cream. My wife will quietly rage on someone (to me, after-the-fact) if they talk about Ted Drewe's having the best "Ice cream." Homie's about to get stabbed with a spoon.

Official Review:

DQ has the best ice cream, but Andy's has the best frozen custard.
